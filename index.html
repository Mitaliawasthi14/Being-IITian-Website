About Us

Being IITian is a student-led collective from premier institutes (IITs, NITs, IIITs), united by a single goal — guiding JEE aspirants through structured mentorship, peer groups, and quality content. We’re not a commercial brand—we’re motivated by community and real impact.

We hold monthly training sessions for our mentors, publish strategy blogs, run live Q&A workshops and release “focus playlists” for exam-fed concentration sessions.
